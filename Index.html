Meet the Founder

The visionary behind FirstStep USA and our commitment to student success

Hello, I'm Maulik Ramnani

As an international student who has navigated the challenges of studying abroad firsthand, I founded FirstStep USA to create the support system I wish I had when I first arrived in the United States.

My Journey

Coming from India to pursue my Master's degree at Syracuse University, I experienced the struggles of finding housing, managing finances, and adapting to a new culture. These challenges inspired me to build a platform that helps students overcome these obstacles more easily.

Why This Project?

I believe every international student deserves a smooth transition to life in the U.S. FirstStep USA combines my technical expertise with my personal experience to provide practical solutions that address real student needs.
